Calcualates an equation and then evalulates it into a single number.
This program follows the rules of order of operations and bracket support.

Usage: advanced-calculator [equations] [options]

Arguements:
  [equations] ... A list of equations to evalulate

Options:
  -h, --help
    Shows this message
  -v, --verbose
    Shows the workings of this program
      --no-eval
    Doesn't evalulate the equations, if the `--verbose` option isn't choosen
    then just prints out the expressions in the calculations.

Example:
  1.5 * (2 + 2) would evalulate to 6.

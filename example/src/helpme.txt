Calculates an equation and then evaluates it into a single number.
This program follows the rules of order of operations and bracket support.

Usage: simp-cal [equations] [options]

Arguments:
  [equations] ... A list of equations to evaluate

Options:
  -v, --version
    Prints the programs version
  -h, --help
    Shows this message
  -V, --verbose
    Shows the workings of this program
      --no-eval
    Doesn't evaluate the equations, if the `--verbose` option isn't chosen
    then just prints out the expressions in the calculations.

Example:
  1.5 * (2 + 2) would evaluate to 6.
